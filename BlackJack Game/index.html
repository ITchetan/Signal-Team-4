    • The object for the player is to draw cards totaling closer to 21, without going over. The cards 2 through 10 are worth their face value. Kings, Queens and Jacks are each worth 10 and aces may be used as either 1 or 11. To achieve this first click the Deal button to be dealt two cards to the players hand. Click the Hit button for additional cards or click Stand if no further cards are required. If stand is selected the computer will be dealt two cards and the scores will be compared and the result will be displayed.
